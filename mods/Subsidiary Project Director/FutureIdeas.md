# Subsidiary Project Director - Future Ideas

Ideas for future updates that could make the mod more fun and deeper.

## Best Additions

- **Project Queue**: Assign a subsidiary its next 2-3 games instead of only one.
- **Creative Brief Mode**: Choose a high-level direction like "safe sequel," "experimental new IP," "mobile cash grab," or "prestige game," then let the subsidiary fill in the details.
- **Parent Company Approval**: Let subsidiaries propose projects that the player can approve, edit, or reject.
- **Budget Control**: Set low, normal, or high budget, affecting quality, risk, development time, and monthly costs.
- **Development Milestones**: Get updates at 25%, 50%, and 75%, with choices like "add polish," "change platforms," or "rush release."
- **Subsidiary Specialization**: Let each subsidiary become stronger at certain genres, topics, platforms, or game sizes over time.
- **Autonomy Level**: Choose how independent each subsidiary is:
  - Full control
  - Approve only
  - Fully autonomous

## IP Features

- **Transfer IP Before Project**: Let the parent company loan or sell an IP to a subsidiary for a sequel/remaster.
- **Co-Owned IPs**: Let the parent company and subsidiary share ownership of a franchise.
- **Spin-Off Requests**: Let a subsidiary request permission to make a spin-off from one of the player's IPs.
- **IP Strategy Screen**: Show all subsidiary-owned IPs, fame, sales, review history, and suggested next games.

## Business And RPG Flavor

- **Studio Identity**: Let subsidiaries develop a personality, such as RPG specialists, budget studio, mobile experts, or prestige developers.
- **Director Traits**: Assign a studio head with bonuses and downsides.
- **Pitch Meetings**: Add event popups where subsidiaries pitch game ideas.
- **Performance Contracts**: Add bonuses if subsidiaries hit review or sales goals.
- **Risky Ambition Projects**: Add expensive dream games with higher review potential but larger failure risk.

## Favorite Next Update Candidate

The strongest next update idea is **Project Queue + Creative Brief Mode**.

This would make subsidiaries feel more alive without forcing the player to micromanage every slider every time. The player could either fully design the next game, or give a high-level creative direction and let the subsidiary handle the rest.
